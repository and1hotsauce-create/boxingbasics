Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need boxing experience?

Not at all. Boxing Basics is built for beginners. You'll learn everything from scratch.

What do I need to bring?

Bring your own wraps and gloves. If you don't have gloves yet, you can borrow a pair from me.

Can I train with a friend?

Yes — group sessions are available at a discounted rate. Get in touch for pricing.

What if I buy a package and want to stop?

No worries — you'll get your money back for any remaining lessons.

Where do sessions take place?

Outdoors in local parks or in a private garage setting around Strathmore & Essendon.
